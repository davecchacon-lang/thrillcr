Common questions

Everything providers ask us.

When and how do I get paid? ▼
Every Monday we transfer your earnings from the previous week directly to your Costa Rican bank account or via SINPE Móvil. No delays, no minimums.
What happens if a guest cancels? ▼
You choose your own cancellation policy — flexible (full refund up to 24h before), moderate (50% refund up to 5 days before) or strict (no refund). We enforce it on your behalf.
Can I control my availability and pricing? ▼
100%. Through your provider dashboard you can block dates, adjust pricing for peak seasons, set group size limits and pause your listing anytime.
I'm already on Viator or GetYourGuide. Can I join? ▼
Absolutely. No exclusivity required. ThrillCR is an additional channel to your existing platforms — and we specialize in the Costa Rica market, so you reach different travelers.
How long does setup take? ▼
Most providers go live within 3–5 business days after applying. We handle the listing page, description and photos — you just send us your content and we take it from there.
What if I have a problem with a guest? ▼
Our support team handles all guest disputes, complaints and refund requests. You focus on delivering the experience — we handle the rest.
